Welcome to Blairsville Fleatique! We opened our doors on November 1, 2018 and have been steadily growing with vendors since day one.

We have a large inventory of antiques, collectibles, DVDs, vinyl records, tools, neon signs, leather goods and home decor just to name a few.

It is easy to become a vendor in our store. We do all of the selling for you. All you need to do is price your items and set up your booth! Booth pricing starts at just $60 per month.

Come on in and see why Blairsville Fleatique is becoming one of the premiere fleatiques in the area!

We are open 7 days a week from 11:00 am to 6:00 pm and are located at 855 Route 22 Hwy West (across from Tri-Star Motors).
